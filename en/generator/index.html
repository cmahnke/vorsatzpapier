Create your own patterns

On this page, the endpapers and wallpapers can be arranged into large-scale patterns to be used as screen backgrounds, for example.

The software is called Lucienne and is intended to commemorate the designer Lucienne Day, who also became famous for her textile and wallpaper designs of the 50s and 60s. More in this blog post.
